Help

  1. Click the slider if the bill/coin is available to give. Red means you don't have the bill/coin, and green means you do have it.
  2. Enter the change due in the “Enter change…” box.
    1. The middle of the box shows the type of bill/coin to give change.
    2. On the right hand side of each box, the number of bills/coins needed to give to the customer will be displayed.
      1. In order to show everything, click the “Show All” button
      2. The leftover change will be displayed at the bottom.

  3. In order to reuse the calculator, re-enter a new number and click “Calculate” again.
